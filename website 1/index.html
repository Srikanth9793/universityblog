Intermediate

The Intermediate Education, also known as Higher Secondary Classes (HSC), is a two-year course conducted by the Andhra Pradesh Board of Intermediate Education. Is one of State secondary education boards of India. The board currently offers 85 streams and courses. BIEAP offers general and vocational groups.

Degree

A degree course is usually a 3 to 4 year long course conferred by a UGC-recognised university, after the completion of a course. ... Only a university can confer a degree. To pursue higher education, a degree from only a UGC approved university is accepted.

Post Graduation

Generally, a postgraduate degree is a degree which you study for once you have finished a bachelor's degree. Some postgraduate degrees require the completion of particular bachelor's degree, others don't. ... Many postgraduate courses are studied at university, but some courses are taught in a commercial environment.
